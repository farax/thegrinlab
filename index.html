How it works
From signals
to decisions
No months-long implementation. No custom scripting. Connect your tools and Meridian starts learning immediately.
01

Connect your tools

Plug in Git, Slack, Jira, Confluence, and Google Calendar. Most integrations configured in minutes. Meridian runs entirely in your VPC — your data never leaves your infrastructure.

02

Vocabulary bootstraps automatically

Meridian scans your existing tools, extracts your component names, team names, and product areas, and builds a vocabulary map of your org. No manual configuration required.

03

Intelligence surfaces to managers

Summaries, risk alerts, and opportunity proposals arrive via Slack and Confluence — the tools you already use. Your engineers keep working exactly as before.
